Built for every role
in your barangay.

Whether you're a resident, a barangay officer, or the captain — BarangayConnect has a place for you. Hover over a role to learn more.

Resident

Your barangay,
in your pocket.

Stay informed, access services, and participate in your community — all without lining up at the barangay hall.

  • Request documents online
  • Report concerns with photo proof
  • Receive real-time emergency alerts
  • Join community events & polls
  • Browse the local services directory
Create an Account

Account approval is handled by your barangay at their own pace.

Officer

Serve your community
more effectively.

Officers get dedicated tools to manage concerns, post announcements, and coordinate with fellow officials.

  • Manage and respond to resident concerns
  • Post announcements & advisories
  • Access an officer-level dashboard
  • View resident reports and updates
  • Coordinate with the barangay captain
Register as an Officer

Officer accounts require authorization from your barangay captain before access is granted.

Captain Admin

Run your barangay
on one platform.

Captains can register their barangay and unlock the full suite of management tools — from resident verification to community analytics.

  • Register and onboard your barangay
  • Full admin dashboard for all features
  • Verify residents and approve accounts
  • Manage officers and their access levels
  • Monitor reports & community analytics
Apply for Your Barangay

Admin accounts require barangay registration and are reviewed before approval.
