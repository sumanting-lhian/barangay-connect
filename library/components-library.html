Alert Banner

.alert-banner

Full-width dismissible banners. Use --orange for weather/advisory, --red for emergencies, --green for success, --blue for info.

All variants

Weather Advisory: Signal No. 1 in effect. Stay indoors and monitor official updates from PAGASA.

Emergency Alert: Mandatory evacuation in low-lying areas of Purok 3.

Update: Flood waters in Barangay Bancod have receded. Normal activities may resume.

Reminder: Barangay ID renewal starts Monday. Bring 2 valid government IDs.

<div class="alert-banner alert-banner--orange" role="alert">
  <i data-lucide="alert-triangle"></i>
  <p><strong>Weather Advisory:</strong> Signal No. 1 in effect.</p>
  <button class="alert-banner__close" aria-label="Dismiss">
    <i data-lucide="x"></i>
  </button>
</div>
<!-- Variants: alert-banner--orange / --red / --green / --blue -->

/* Dismiss JS */
document.querySelectorAll('.alert-banner__close').forEach(btn => {
  btn.addEventListener('click', () => btn.closest('.alert-banner').remove());
});

Notice Banner

.notice-banner

A card-like inline notice for important callouts within page content. More prominent than .alert-notice, less intrusive than a full alert banner.

Report incidents confidentially to our Youth Protection Officer.

Curfew in effect: 10PM – 5AM

Per Barangay Resolution No. 2024-015. Violators will be reported.

Your document request is under review.

Processing takes 1–3 business days. You'll receive an SMS update.

Your concern has been resolved.

Thank you for reporting. The issue was addressed on April 2, 2025.

  </label>
</div>
